  BetterApp - Installation Instructions
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

If macOS shows a security warning, follow these steps:

1. Open System Settings (⚙️ icon in your Dock)
2. Click "Privacy & Security"
3. Scroll down to the "Security" section
4. You'll see "BetterApp was blocked"
5. Click "Open Anyway"
6. Click "Open" in the confirmation dialog

Then:
7. Drag BetterApp to your Applications folder
8. Open BetterApp from Applications

This is normal for indie apps - macOS just needs confirmation
that the app is safe. BetterApp is completely safe to use.
